PGL CS2 MAJOR COPENHAGEN 2024

EMPRESA

The Royal Arena in Copenhagen, Denmark, will play host to the PGL CS2 Major Copenhagen playoffs, a 16,000 capacity indoor arena situated in the Ørestad South area of the city. The €134 million venue has hosted a number of elite Counter-Strike tournaments in the past, and has also hosted a range of other spectacles like music concerts, sporting championships, and other esports events.

Denmark has produced a plethora of Counter-Strike stars across the game’s 20+ year history, and Danish players and teams have claimed numerous accolades and awards in that time. From the exploits of mTw in Counter-Strike 1.6 to the dominant era of Astralis in Global Offensive, Danes have always been at the heart of competitive Counter-Strike. The most successful player and coach of all time in CS:GO Majors, dupreeh and zonic respectively, both hail from Denmark.
